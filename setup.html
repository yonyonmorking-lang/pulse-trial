Precision rhythm challenge

Pulse Trial

A precision rhythm memory game. Listen to short wooden pulse patterns, then recreate them through your microphone as accurately as you can across five rounds.

Before we start

Is this your first time playing?

New players get a quick explanation. Returning players can jump straight into setup.

How to play

Listen first. Tap only on TAP NOW.

1 Each round opens on its own page and starts with 3, 2, 1, GO.
2 The wooden rhythm plays twice. Do not tap during LISTEN.
3 When TAP NOW appears, recreate the rhythm near your microphone.
4 After each round, you see only that round score. The average appears after round 5.

Rhythm accuracy lab

Hear the rhythm. Match it exactly.

Each round opens on its own page, plays the wooden pattern twice, then measures timing, speed, tap count, and strength with deterministic scoring.
