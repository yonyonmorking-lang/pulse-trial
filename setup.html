Rhythm Game

A rhythm memory game. Hear a wooden pattern twice, then copy it by tapping your phone, clicking your mouse, or knocking on a table across five rounds.

Before we start

Is this your first time playing?

New players get a quick explanation. Returning players can jump straight into setup.

How to play

Hear it twice. Then copy it.

1 Each round starts with 3, 2, 1, GO.
2 The game plays one wooden rhythm two times. Just listen during both plays.
3 When TAP NOW appears, copy the rhythm with phone taps, mouse clicks, or table knocks.
4 The game scores how close your timing, speed, tap count, and strength were.
